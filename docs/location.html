Hotel Henry

Hotel Henry is located at 444 Forest Ave, Buffalo NY, 14213. The entrance is off of Rockwell Rd. Free valet service will be provided.

Coming From Cuba

If you are coming from Cuba, here is our recommended set of directions.

  1. Take 16 N to 400 N
  2. Take 400 N to I-190 N
  3. Take I-190 N to NY-198 Scajauda Expressway
  4. Take the Grant St exit
  5. Turn left onto Letchworth Rd
  6. Continue onto Rockwell Rd
  7. Look for sign for Hotel Henry entrance on right
